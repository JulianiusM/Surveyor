## Access Options

Surveyor offers two ways to participate:

### Registered User Account
- Create and manage your own surveys, events, packing lists, activities, and drivers lists
- Access all your items from one dashboard
- Full control over permissions and sharing

### Guest Access
- Participate without creating an account
- Vote on surveys, sign up for activities, mark packing items, register as driver
- Use personalized links provided by the organizer
- **Important:** Save your personalized link to access your submissions again

## Creating an Account

### Registration Process

1. **Go to Registration Page**
   - Click "Register" on the homepage
   - Or visit `/users/register`

2. **Fill in Required Information**
   - **Username** (login name, unique)
   - **Display name** (how your name appears to others)
   - **Email** (must be valid)
   - **Password** (minimum 8 characters)
   - **Repeat password** (must match)

3. **Submit Registration**
   - Click "Register" button
   - You'll be logged in automatically

### Alternative: OIDC Login

If your organization has configured OIDC (Single Sign-On):
- Click "Login using [Provider Name]"
- Authenticate with your organization credentials
- Account created automatically

### Password Requirements

Your password must be at least 8 characters long. The form provides real-time feedback as you type.

## Logging In

### Standard Login

1. **Navigate to Login Page**
   - Click "Login" on homepage
   - Or visit `/users/login`

2. **Enter Credentials**
   - Username or email
   - Password

3. **Submit**
   - Click "Login"
   - Redirected to your dashboard

### Forgot Password?

1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check email for reset link
4. Follow link to create new password

## Guest Access

Guest access lets you participate without registering.

### How Guest Access Works

1. **Receive Invitation**
   - Organizer shares a link to a survey, activity, packing list, or drivers list
   - Link includes entity details

2. **Register as Guest**
   - Click the link
   - See two options:
     - **Log in** (if you have an account)
     - **Create account** (to register)
     - **Continue as guest** (below the divider)

3. **Fill Guest Form**
   - **Name** (how you'll appear to others)
   - **Email** (optional, for notifications)

4. **Submit**
   - You'll receive a **personalized link**
   - **Save this link!** It's your only way to access your responses again

### Important About Guest Links

✅ **Do:**
- Save your personalized link
- Bookmark it in your browser
- Keep the email with your link

❌ **Don't:**
- Lose your personalized link (you can't recover it)
- Share your link (it's personal to you)

### What Guests Can Do

Guests can participate in:
- **Surveys** - Vote on date combinations
- **Activity Plans** - Sign up for activity slots
- **Packing Lists** - Mark items you'll bring
- **Drivers Lists** - Register as driver or passenger

Guests **cannot**:
- Create new entities
- See a dashboard of all activities
- Change permissions

## Common Questions

**Q: What's the difference between username and display name?**  
A: Username is for logging in (must be unique). Display name is what others see (can be anything).

**Q: Can I use the same email for multiple guest participations?**  
A: Yes, but you'll receive a different personalized link for each activity.

**Q: Can I convert my guest participation to a registered account?**  
A: Yes, create an account and contact the organizer to link your guest data.

**Q: I lost my guest link. Can I recover it?**  
A: No, guest links cannot be recovered. You'll need to register as a new guest if needed.

**Q: Can I change my password?**  
A: Yes, use "Forgot Password" on the login page to reset it.

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Register

**Problem:** Registration form shows errors

**Solutions:**
- Ensure username is not already taken
- Check email is valid
- Verify passwords match
- Password must be at least 8 characters

### Can't Login

**Problem:** Login fails

**Solutions:**
- Verify username/email spelling
- Check password (case-sensitive)
- Try password reset if unsure
- Contact administrator if issues persist

### Guest Link Not Working

**Problem:** Personalized link doesn't load

**Solutions:**
- Check link is complete (not truncated)
- Try copying/pasting instead of clicking
- Check if activity still exists
- Contact organizer
